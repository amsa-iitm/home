  • Industrial Talk - Exploring the Power of Ansys Multiphysics: From Simulation to Digital Twin Innovation by CADFEM India

    24th April 2025

    Prof. Philippe Odier from the Department of Physics, ENS Lyon, France presented the work done on selection of textiles for the suits used by cyclists at the 2024 Paris Olympics. He also explained various scenarios of 'drafting,' which refers to the reduction in effort experienced by a cyclist when riding behind another. The talk concluded with a discussion on the extraction of the instantaneous positions of the riders on the track during a race using automatic video tracking.

  • 42 Explained! - Combinatorial Designs: From Agriculture to Quantum Mechanics by Prof. Arul Lakshminarayan

    26th February 2025

The talk culminated in a recent result showing that a quantum solution exists even for the unsolvable case of order 6.
