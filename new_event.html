AMBE RESEARCH PALOOZA 2024

The department's Annual Research Scholars' day returns this year in a new Avatar! Let us celebrate our passion, dedication and innovation in research with a day filled with inspiring presentations, posters, and a workshop; all following an electrifying plenary talk.

Date: 5th July, 2024

Time: 9am - 5pm

Venue: ICSR Hall


REGISTER HERE to attend and for the lunch!

REGISTRATION IS CLOSED!! THANK YOU FOR YOUR RESPONSE.

This page contains all the relevant announcements, links and the schedule. Keep scrolling to know more about the event and how to participate.



INFORMATION FOR PARTICPANTS


Are you interested in sharing your work at the AMBE research Palooza? You are invited to submit a short video (2-3 min) showcasing your research.


Why Participate?
  • An opportunity to network with your peers
  • Cash Prizes worth Rs.5000 and Rs.2500
  • Certificates for all participants
  • Showcasing your work on our social media handles
How to Participate?
  • Create a short (2-3 minute) video - clearly presenting your research, its significance, and your findings. You may refer to the examples here and here
  • Upload your video to google drive
  • Fill your details and preference for your presentation format, BEFORE 21st June, end of the day in this Google Form
  • Note that the talk event is open only for PhD students, other students can only present a poster
  • Note that this form is only for the presentations. To register as a participant and confirm your presence for lunch, use the form in the Register button above
  • A Faculty panel will evaulate your video and make a decision
  • Selected PhD students will be invited for a 20 minute talk while others (including MS and MTech) will be invited to display their poster
