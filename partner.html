Companies We've Collaborated With

Partnering with industry leaders to create real-world opportunities for our students.

ISRO

Indian Space Research Organisation, India's national space agency, known for satellite launches, lunar and interplanetary missions (like Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan), and advancing space technology for scientific and societal applications.

Seimens

A global technology company specializing in industrial automation, digitalization, electrification, and engineering solutions across manufacturing, energy, healthcare, and infrastructure sectors.

Danfoss

A Danish multinational engineering company focused on energy-efficient solutions in areas like refrigeration, air conditioning, heating, and mobile hydraulics, driving innovation in climate and energy technologies.

Perfint Healthcare

A medical technology company specializing in robotic and navigation systems for interventional oncology and radiology, enabling precise, minimally invasive diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.
