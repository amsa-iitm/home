About Us

Who we are and what we stand for.

A student-run association that strengthens the academic, professional, and social life of Applied Mechanics students at IIT Madras.

WorkshopsResearch TalksMentorshipCommunity

Our Vision

A community that grows through shared knowledge.

AMSA exists to help students interact with one another, learn from one another, and grow as a department community. The association creates student-friendly spaces for conversations that range from research questions to career choices and campus life.

Our focus is simple: empower students, connect people across batches and disciplines, and build leadership through meaningful participation.

What We Do

Programs with academic and social value.

Academic Exchange

Seminars, chalk talks, research showcases, and publishing discussions.

Professional Exposure

Industry visits, alumni interaction, career guidance, and partner programs.

Student Life

Sports, informal sessions, mentorship, wellness support, and community events.
