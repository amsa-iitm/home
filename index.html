About AMSA

A student-run association for learning, collaboration, and belonging.

The Applied Mechanics Students Association is a student-led group that brings together students across batches to share knowledge, build skills, and learn from each other's academic and professional journeys.

Through workshops, seminars, guest lectures, industrial visits, research events, and informal gatherings, AMSA creates spaces where students can connect with peers, faculty, alumni, and industry professionals.
